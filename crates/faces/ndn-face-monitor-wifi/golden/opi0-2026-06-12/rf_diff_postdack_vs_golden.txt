RF_PATH_A registers that differ: our post-DACK userspace state vs the golden
fully-calibrated (kernel) chip, ch161/BW20. Excludes RF reg 0x00 (not validly
readable via the direct BB window) and 0x18/0x1a (channel/BW, which match).
These 32 registers are the cumulative result of the unported IQK/LCK/DPK/TSSI
calibrations — the remaining on-air gate. Most deltas are small (fine tuning);
none is a single obvious TX-enable. Use as the per-register cross-check when
porting the heavy cals.

Also note RF reg 0x00 (RF mode/standby): golden reads 0x31d32 via the kernel's
indirect RF read; our direct-window read returns 0x10000 (likely an invalid
read of reg 0, not a true mismatch). Worth confirming with a proper indirect
RF-reg-0 read when porting LCK/the RF mode setup.
